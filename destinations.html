Destinations

Maasai Mara

The Maasai Mara National Reserve, located in Kenya, is renowned for its vast savannahs, abundant wildlife, and the annual wildebeest migration. Visitors can experience thrilling game drives and witness the stunning beauty of the African landscape

Starting from $1000

Diani Beach

Diani Beach, situated along Kenya's coastline, boasts pristine white sands, turquoise waters, and lush palm trees. It's a popular destination for beach lovers, offering a range of water sports, vibrant nightlife, and luxury resorts.

Starting from $800

Malindi

Malindi is a coastal town known for its rich history, Swahili culture, and beautiful beaches. Visitors can explore ancient ruins, indulge in fresh seafood cuisine, and enjoy water activities like snorkeling and diving in the colorful coral reefs.

Starting from $700

Lamu

Lamu Island, off the coast of Kenya, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famed for its well-preserved Swahili architecture, narrow alleyways, and tranquil atmosphere. Travelers can immerse themselves in the island's unique culture, visit historic mosques, and relax on pristine beaches.

Starting from $900

Mount Kenya Reserve

Mount Kenya National Park is home to the second-highest peak in Africa and offers diverse ecosystems, including glaciers, forests, and alpine meadows. Adventurous trekkers can conquer the summit, while nature enthusiasts can spot rare wildlife like the mountain bongo and giant lobelia

Starting from $1200
